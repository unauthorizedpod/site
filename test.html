About the Show

Film by film, two siblings go through cinema history, from the blockbusters to the arthouse, with discussions on what these movies meant then and how we see them now.


Using the BFI's Sight and Sound list as a starting point, they explore canonical films and the decades when cinema exploded. No film degrees, no press passes, they like it unauthorized.
