Race Details

The 1-mile walk and the 10-mile run will begin at 7am.
The 10k run will begin around 7:50am.

Stroll Along the Canal

The walkers will begin at the First Street starting location with the 10-milers. Their path is an out-and-back that will pass under the Gill-Montague bridge and turn around at the fifth street bridge.

10k All Day

All 10k runners (just Zach) will join the 10-milers on the Connecticut River pedestrian bridge after their turn-around. They will run the same path back.
