Race Details

Stroll Along the Canal

The walkers will begin at the First Street starting location with the 10-milers. Their path is an out-and-back that will pass under the Gill-Montague bridge and turn around at the fifth street bridge.

10k All Day

All 10k runners (just Zach) will start at Connecticut River pedestrian bridge just before the 10m mile 4 marker. They'll run the same path back as the 10-milers.

The ol' 10 Mile

All 10-mile runners will start with the walkers at First Street and then follow these directions:

  • 0.0 Run until the trail ends at Depot Street.
  • 2.3 Take a right onto Montague City Road
  • 2.6 Take a right on Greenfield Road
  • 2.8 Take a right on the Canalside Rail Trail
  • 3.4 Turn around at the corner and run along the trail until it ends at Masonic Avenue. 10k runners (Zach) will join on bridge (and guide you the rest of the way).
  • 4.2 Turn right on Montague City Road
  • 5.6
    Turn left on 11th Street 💧 A water station will be set up here just before the bridge.
  • 5.7 Turn left on G Street and run until trail starts.
  • 6.7 Turn right onto wooded trail
  • 7.6 Follow wooded trail until I Street and take a left.
  • 7.8 Follow I Street to Tenth Street
  • 7.9 Turn right on G Street and left on 11th Street.
  • 8.0
    Cross the bridge and turn left on trail. 💧 A water station will be set up here.
  • 8.8 Follow trail until Great Falls Discovery Center.
  • 9.2 Turn Right in parking area and cross the Gill-Montague Bridge
  • 9.5 Turn around at the end of the bridge, then turn left at the Montague Town Hall and reconnect with the trail
  • 10.0 Finish at the Unity Park parking lot!
